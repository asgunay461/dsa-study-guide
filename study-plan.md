# 4-Week DSA Study Plan

## Week 1: Fundamentals & Arrays
- Review data structure basics (Wikipedia summary).
- Study arrays, implementation, common operations.
- Practice array problems on GFG.
- Quick revision of time/space complexity.

## Week 2: Linked Lists, Stacks & Queues
- Understand singly, doubly, circular linked lists.
- Implement stack and queue using arrays & linked lists.
- Solve linked‑list, stack, queue problems.

## Week 3: Trees & Heaps
- Binary trees, BST, AVL, heap concepts.
- Traversals (in‑order, pre‑order, post‑order, level‑order).
- Practice tree and heap problems.

## Week 4: Graphs & Advanced Topics
- Graph representations, BFS, DFS, shortest‑path basics.
- Intro to advanced structures: Trie, Segment Tree (overview).
- Comprehensive mock exam and review.

Each week allocate:
- 2 days theory reading (Wikipedia + GFG).
- 3 days coding practice.
- 1 day revision & notes.
- 1 day break / light review.

Resources:
- Wikipedia article on Data Structures.
- GeeksforGeeks tutorials and quizzes.
- Personal `research-notes.md` for reference.
